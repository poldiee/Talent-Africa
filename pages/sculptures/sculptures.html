Africa
is known for its imaginative sculptures. Made by skilled craftsmen, the sculptures represent artistic visions, symbols, and stories.

Here are a Few Highlights from Sculptures Artists.

At Talent Africa

Sudi is a seasoned sculptor from Nanyuki, Kenya. He assembles metal, welding and fitting together creating amazing artwork

Husda is a sculptor from Turkana, Kenya. She is a modelling sculptor mostly using clay to mold nd shape into different beautiful shapes.

Tunu is a sculptor from Ikolomani, Kenya. She is an inspiring talent as she adds different malleable items to a work to create really beautiful forms.

Babu, a scuptor from Kenya deals with wood carvings. He mainly plies his trade in Maasai Mara where he normally displays his collection for exhibition.

Ngurumo is a talented craftsman from Uganda. He is a modelling sculptor mainly creating artefacts which display the nature of the African continent.
